Note on Image Availability

The images on this page were found using filters on Google and other websites for free alteration and commercial usage. I particularly utilized filters to make sure I could use the images for this website without any issues, but none of the images have been altered.

While images for some people appeared with these filters, photos for others did not. Other photographs may exist on websites that do not allow free use, so they are not featured here. I wanted to be cautious when looking for photographs to avoid copyright violation.
